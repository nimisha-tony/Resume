MCA: Main & Mini Project

Resto Table & Meal Booking

The website facilitated table reservations and meal pre-orders for customers at a restaurant, ensuring timely and accurate service. By managing bookings for specific dates and times, it enhanced both the dining experience and operational efficiency. This involved organizing customer preferences and special requests, allowing the kitchen and service staff to prepare in advance. The system contributed to a smoother workflow, minimized wait times, and increased customer satisfaction. Effective communication with both customers and staff was key, ensuring each reservation was handled with precision and care, leading to an overall improved dining experience.

Front end: Django | Back end: SQLite

Flood Relief System

Our flood relief system website provides a comprehensive platform for managing and coordinating flood relief efforts. Users can access real-time information on affected areas, relief camps, and required resources. The system allows individuals to register as volunteers or donors, enabling seamless coordination of aid distribution. Through interactive maps and intuitive interfaces, users can locate and contribute to relief efforts efficiently. Additionally, administrative features facilitate the management of relief operations, including resource allocation and volunteer coordination.
